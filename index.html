Welcome to La Casa, Napier

Located in Napier's beautiful Art Deco district, La Casa provides boutique accommodation in a quiet street near the city centre. The Art Deco villa was built in 1930s, in the midst of the Art Deco period, and offers garden and city views across Marewa, also known as the Art Deco suburb, with many examples of the architectural style around the nearby streets. Visitors can enjoy comfortable and spacious bedrooms upstairs, free wifi, a lovely and secluded garden and patio, off-street parking (for two cars) and easy access to the city centre and the beach - 2 mins drive, or 15 mins walk to the city.



Within a few km are the lovely port area of Ahuriri bustling with bars and restaurants, the city centre featuring many Art Deco buildings, McLeans Park, Napier beach, museum, the Six Sisters, the National Aquarium, Centre for Arts Napier (CAN), the Botanical Gardens, the Soundshell and gardens, Pania of the Reef (statue) and many other local attractions. The airport is 5km away.




Guests can relax in the visitors' lounge area with fridge, toaster and kettle, and enjoy tea and coffee, fresh fruit, toast, butter and condiments for breakfast.
