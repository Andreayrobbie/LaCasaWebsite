Welcome to La Casa, Napier

Located in Napier's beautiful Art Deco district, La Casa provides boutique accommodation in a quiet street near the city centre. The house was built in 1936, in the midst of the Art Deco period, and offers garden views, free wifi, off-street parking and a secluded picnic area. Within a few km are the lovely port area of Ahuriri bustling with bars, the city centre featuring many Art Deco buildings, McLeans Park, Napier beach and the Pania of the Reef statue. The airport is 5km away. Guests can use a small lounge area with fridge, toaster and kettle, and enjoy tea and coffee, toast, butter and jam for breakfast.
